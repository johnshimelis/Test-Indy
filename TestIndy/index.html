Use of cookies on our Webites

A cookie is a very small test file placed on your hard drive by a Web page server. It is your identification card, and cannot be executed as code or deliver viruses. You are sent cookies whenever you surf our Web sites or request information. Most browsers automatically accept cookies. You may adjust your preferences on your computer to refuse cookies or accept certain cookies. If you set your preferences to turn off cookies, you may not be able to view certain features of our Web sites. 

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of your information, or about this privacy statement, please e-mail the webmaster.

We Protect Your Privacy

This privacy statement covers the collection and use of Web site data for the Health & Hospital Corporation of Marion County (HHC), Marion County Public Health Department (MCPHD) and Eskenazi Health (EH).

Personally identifiable information

Personally identifiable information is specific information about visitors to our Web site. The personally identifiable information that we may collect when you use any of our sites include your name, mailing address, phone number(s), e-mail address and identification numbers. We may request this information from you when you request a service, request informational materials or contact us. HHC, MCPHD and EH will release personally identifiable information about you as required to do so by law, subpoena, court order or legal process. HHC, MCPHD and EH will not rent, sell or give away your personally identifiable information entered into their Web sites to third parties for marketing or mailing list purposes. However, your personally identifiable information may be shared as required by the Indiana Public Records Act. We are required to maintain documents under the Indiana Access to Public Records Act, but we do not collect personal information other than to respond to you or for other purposes required by law.
