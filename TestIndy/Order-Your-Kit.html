You will Know Your Status

Test in the privacy of your own home. How awesome is that!

A skilled health educator on a virtual call will walk you through the test and your result.

You will receive a $25 gift card for allowing our educator to assist you with taking the test virtually.

Ordering a Self-Test Kit

In a few days, you will receive a discreet package in the mail that will contain your testing kit.

Please allow one of our health educators to walk through the process with you in performing the test and sharing the sample.

We will review your individual result together, which allows you to receive a $25 gift card in the mail.

Thank You for allowing us to guide you through the testing process.

How to Be eligible

  • You must be a Marion County Resident
  • You must be 17 years of age or older
  • It must be at least 90 days since you last tested
